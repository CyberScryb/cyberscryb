Frequently Asked Questions

Title tags are a confirmed ranking factor. Meta descriptions don't directly affect rankings but significantly impact click-through rates, which indirectly affect rankings.

What are Open Graph tags?

Open Graph tags (og:title, og:description, og:image) control how your page appears when shared on Facebook, LinkedIn, and other social platforms.
